# aignite
Tool for internal Client Use. 

Structure: 
1. Goals
2. Brand Voice
3. Features & Benefits 
4. Segments
5. Customer Journeys
6. Personalization
7. Copywriting
8. Email Design Brief
9. Campaign Calendar
10. Analyze Performance - make adjsutments to copy, calendar, offers, etc.
11. Analyze Deliverability - 
12. A/B Testing 

Specific Verticals: 
1. Supplemants
2. Food & Beverage
3. Apparel
4. General

Design Documents 
Set the overall design rules and definitions. 

Brand Voice 
Set the the creative parameters for creating email copy and designs. 

Features and Benefits 
Outline the unique selling points and features and benefits for the client's brand. 

Campaign Calender 
To do research and craft recommendations 13 week promotional calendar that brings in any relevant social or national events or holidays to leverage in the messasaging or construction of emails and/or promotions. Allow for the addition of vertical or brand specific dates and events to be included in the calendar recommendations.
